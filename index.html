Hi, my name is Kam Hin (Eric) Cheung. I'm a freelance 3-D artist and graphic designer who currently resides in New Jersey. Thank you for stopping by and checking out my portfolio website.

I have over 10+ years of experience in pre-press and print/digital production. Currently, I work full-time as a pre-press assistant at a magazine publishing company in NYC. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design degree from Monclair State University.

My main interest right now is in 3D modeling. I got interested in 3D modeling about 6 years ago after I learned my favorite manga (Japanese style comics) uses 3D software to "draw" the background.  At that time, I was planning to create a manga of my own so I thought it would be a useful skill to learn. I started  by watching tutorials on Youtube and using a 3D software called Blender. I further developed my skills by taking a couple online courses and trying out other programs such as 3D Max and Unreal Engine. My preferred program is Blender and architecture visualizing is the area I'm most knowledgeable and interested in.

This portfolio was built using Adobe Muse, Adobe Edge Anime and Photoshop. I'm currently working to expand my web design and development abilities to include mastering Ruby on Rails.

My other interests are painting, photography and drumming.
